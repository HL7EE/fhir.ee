Resource Profile: EEBase EpisodeOfCare

Official URL: https://fhir.ee/StructureDefinition/ee-episode-of-care Version: 0.9.0
Draft as of 2023-05-12 Computable Name: EEBaseEpisodeOfCare

An association between a patient and an organization / healthcare provider(s) during which time encounters may occur. The managing organization assumes a level of responsibility for the patient during this time.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: EEBase Encounter
